Platform Strategy

From campus trading tool to AI-powered commerce hub

This concept combines student-to-student resale, merchant advertising, verified trust, and AI-assisted operations in one focused campus product.

01

Verified users enter

Students, advertisers, tutors, recruiters and nearby merchants join with role-based access.

→
02

Items or ads are created

Physical goods, digital services, job posts and sponsored campaigns all enter structured flows.

→
03

AI improves quality

AI helps with copy, pricing, review, matching, first-response customer service and push targeting.

→
04

Meetup and follow-up

Users coordinate time and location, receive reminders, complete the handoff, and leave ratings.

Layer 1

User Layer

Buyers, sellers, advertisers and admins share one identity and credit framework built on campus verification.

Layer 2

Gateway Layer

API gateway, H5 client, mini-app entry and notification channels route every request safely into the platform.

Layer 3

Core Business

Listings, transactions, user center, ad system, ratings, compliance and payment escrow form the product core.

Layer 4

AI Capability

AI fill-in, pricing review, matching engine and RAG support reduce friction while improving trust and efficiency.

Layer 5

Infrastructure

MySQL, Redis, object storage, message queues and analytics keep the platform responsive, traceable and scalable.

Value Proposition

  • Centralized campus marketplace that removes repeated posting and fragmented searching.
  • AI-assisted listing, review, pricing and communication reduce time cost on both sides.
  • Verified trust, ratings and structured meetup flow make transactions feel safer and clearer.

Revenue Stream

  • Advertising fees from local merchants, recruiters, agencies and service providers.
  • Paid boosting for urgent sellers who want more visibility.
  • Featured placement and premium campaign tools for campus-facing businesses.
  • Optional deposit and escrow-related service fees for higher-trust transactions.

Exclusive Benefit

  • Saves time by concentrating listings, conversations and decision-making in one place.
  • Improves accuracy with AI-generated copy, condition review, pricing reference and targeted matching.
  • Breaks response-time barriers with AI-assisted first replies for merchants and advertisers.
